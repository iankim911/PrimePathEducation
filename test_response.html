About PrimePath

PrimePath is a comprehensive assessment platform designed for K-12 English learners in Korea. Our system helps academies efficiently place students in the appropriate curriculum level through automated testing and grading.

Our Programs:

  • PRIME CORE - Grades 1-4
  • PRIME ASCENT - Grades 5-6
  • PRIME EDGE - Middle School
  • PRIME PINNACLE - High School
