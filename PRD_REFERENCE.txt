1. Executive Summary

Product Vision

PrimePath is a two-part educational assessment platform for Kâ€“12 English learners in Korea.

Module 1: Placement_Test
A web-based assessment tool for prospective students to take level tests online. Teachers upload exam PDFs (scanned), audio files, and answer keys. The system matches test difficulty to the student's input (grade and academic rank). Students may adjust difficulty mid-test via a button (+1 or -1 level). Results are auto-graded (except long answers) and mapped to the appropriate curriculum level.

Module 2: Progress_Tracker (Future Phase)
A logged-in system for enrolled students to take monthly assessments, track improvement, and receive personalized feedback. Teachers can assign exams by class, upload new tests, and view dashboards showing student progress. (Stretch: smart vocab tool, AI-powered diagnostics, mobile interface.)
